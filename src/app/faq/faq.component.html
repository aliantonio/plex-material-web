• closed_captionHow do I turn on subtitles?
    Web Player

    In the bottom-right hand of the player, about the 4th icon from the left is the settings button. Click that, then click on Subtitles. If there are subtitles available, it will say English (SRT). Click on that. If no subtitles are vailable, click on Search. This will attempt to search for subtitles on the web. Find the best one that syncs up with the video you are watching.

    Android/iOS

    On the bottom-right hand of the screen, click on the menu button (probably the icon with the 3 dots). A card will pop up. Click on Playback Settings. Then click on Subtitles. If there are subtitles available, it will say English (SRT). Click on that. If no subtitles are vailable, click on More. This will attempt to search for subtitles on the web. Find the best one that syncs up with the video you are watching.

  • theatersHow do I fix poor image quality or buffering?
    Web Player

    In the bottom-right hand of the player, about the 4th icon from the left is the settings button. Click that, then click on Quality. Ensure that "Convert automatically" is checked. This will usually provide the best setting based on your connection speed. If you're still having issues, click on "Show All", and then click a lower quality setting.

    Android/iOS

    On the bottom-right hand of the screen, click on the menu button (probably the icon with the 3 dots). A card will pop up. Click on Playback Settings. Then click on Quality. Ensure that "Convert automatically" is checked. This will usually provide the best setting based on your connection speed. If you're still having issues, click on "Show All", and then click a lower quality setting.
